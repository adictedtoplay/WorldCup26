FAQ

What changed?

This is a clean simplified build. The bracket was removed. The dashboard now focuses on upcoming fixtures, group tables, and qualification predictions.

What data is embedded?

All 72 group fixtures are embedded, including Germany 2–1 Ivory Coast, Ecuador 0–0 Curacao, and Netherlands 5–1 Sweden.

How are predictions calculated?

Predictions are not official. They use current table position, remaining matches, goal difference, and an Elo prior to estimate qualification likelihood.
